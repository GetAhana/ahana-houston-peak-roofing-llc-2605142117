Our Story

After years on large crews where communication broke down, Jordan Alvarez founded Peak Roofing to prove a different way works. A smaller team answers the phone, shows up on time, and explains options without pressure.

Every inspection, every estimate, every install carries the same project lead from start to finish. Your roof is not a commodity—it protects your family and your home's value.
