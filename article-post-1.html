What Your Insurer Needs to See
Storm Damage Claims:

Spring storms bring hail and wind damage to Houston roofs. Learn what photos and documentation your insurer needs, how to file a claim, and when to bring in a licensed contractor to maximize your settlement.

TL;DR

  • File your claim within 24–48 hours and provide daylight photos with scale (coin or ruler) showing hail dents and impact marks
  • Document gutters, vents, flashing, and roof access points where granule loss is visible
  • Hire a licensed contractor to provide professional estimates and support supplements if the adjuster's offer falls short
  • Expect claim resolution in 4–8 weeks; supplement requests may add 2–4 weeks in high-volume storm months
  • Peak Roofing handles documentation, adjuster coordination, and installs so you focus on safety

File a claim right after the storm

Contact your insurance company within 24–48 hours of hail, wind, or storm damage. Insurers monitor claim volume spikes and may deprioritize late reports. Describe the damage briefly (e.g., "hail damage to roof, gutters, and siding") and request an adjuster visit.

Photography matters: What insurers look for

Adjusters assess damage by comparing before-and-after conditions and impact patterns. Take photos in daylight showing hail dents or impact marks with a coin or ruler for scale. Photograph roof access points (gutters, vents, flashing) where granule loss is visible. Include wide shots of your home and close-ups of affected areas—then email or upload them to your claim within the first week.

Coordinate with a licensed contractor early

A reputable Houston roofer like Peak Roofing can document damage professionally, provide repair estimates, and support your claim. We photograph impact patterns, measure affected areas, and explain scope to adjusters in their language. Many insurers welcome a contractor's input because it clarifies the actual cost.

Supplement process and timing

If your adjuster's estimate falls short of the repair cost, file a supplement request within 30–60 days. Peak Roofing provides detailed supplements with itemized costs and labor, photos, and building code notes. Most Houston claims resolve in 4–8 weeks with a supplement; expect occasional delays in high-volume storm months.
