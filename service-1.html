Hail, wind, and water damage demand fast action. We document everything, work directly with insurers, and build roofs to survive Gulf Coast weather.

Storm-repair and replacement specs for Houston durability

  • Impact-resistant shingles (Class 4 rating) or architectural asphalt; metal standing seam up to 140 mph wind rating
  • Ice-and-water shield underlayment in vulnerable zones (eaves, valleys, penetrations)
  • Proper attic ventilation ratio (1 sq ft per 150 sq ft of ceiling) to prevent moisture buildup in Gulf humidity
  • Fastening per current building code (6 fasteners per shingle; metal-to-wood with stainless hardware)

When to call us

Not sure if your roof sustained hail damage? Watch for these warning signs.

  • Missing, cracked, or dented shingles in a pattern across the slope
  • Granule loss (black streaks or bare spots in the shingle surface)
  • Bruising on metal vents, gutters, or chimney flashing
  • Curling or raised shingle edges on slopes that face the storm direction
  • Interior leaks, water stains, or damp attic insulation after heavy rain or hail

FAQ — Storm damage & insurance restoration

Do you handle insurance claims?

Yes. We handle the insurance inspection, document everything with photographs, and file the claim on your behalf when applicable. Our crews are experienced with supplement documentation and adjuster coordination across the Houston metro area.

How quickly can you complete the work after hail?

Most storm-damage jobs complete within 1–2 weeks after approval, depending on material availability and crew scheduling. We prioritize active leaks and post-hail triage same-week when possible.

What warranty do you provide?

We offer a 10-year workmanship warranty on full replacements, plus manufacturer warranties on materials (typically 20–40 years for asphalt shingles, longer for premium metal systems). Both are documented in your final warranty packet.
